ALTogether

BRAND IDENTITY, WEB DESIGN, MERCHANDISE

ALTogether is a brand meant to celebrate all things related to alternative music. Rather than gatekeeping the alt crowd, ALTogether is here to share the ins and outs of the scene. It focuses heavily on creating a stronger connection between alternative music fans.

Inspired by 90s grunge and old record stores, the brand works both in the realm of a printed magazine, as well as a modern, online news source. ALTogether can also be found on social media promoting their latest articles or sharing a fun range of merchandise.
